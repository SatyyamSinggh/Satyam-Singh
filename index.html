IEEE 754 - 32-bit Floating floating-point



What is IEEE 754(32bit)


IEEE 754 is a standard for representing floating-point numbers. The 32-bit format consists of:
  • 1-bit for the sign (0 for positive, 1 for negative)
  • 8-bits for the exponent (with a bias of 127)
  • 23-bits for the fraction (mantissa)
